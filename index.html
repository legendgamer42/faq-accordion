FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers real-world coding challenges to help you improve your frontend development skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s a great way to build a portfolio and learn through practice.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, there’s a free tier that gives you access to a wide range of challenges. They also offer a Pro subscription with premium challenges, design files, and additional features.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! That’s one of the main goals. Completing challenges and showcasing your solutions is a great way to impress potential employers or clients.

How can I get help if I’m stuck on a challenge?

You can join the Frontend Mentor Discord community or browse the solution hub to see how others tackled the same problem. It’s a collaborative and supportive space for learning.
